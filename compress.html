Frequently Asked Questions

Will my PDF lose quality? ▾

With Low compression, quality is nearly identical to the original. Medium and High compression may reduce image resolution slightly, but text always remains crisp and readable.

Is my file safe and private? ▾

Absolutely. All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your file is never uploaded to any server — not ours, not anyone's.

Why is my file not compressing much? ▾

PDFs that are already compressed, or contain mostly text with few images, may not reduce significantly. PDFs with large embedded images show the most savings.

Is there a file size limit? ▾

There's no enforced limit — but very large files (500MB+) may be slow depending on your device's memory and browser capabilities.
